## BRAND OVERVIEW

### Mission
VALE empowers families to make informed funeral decisions at their most vulnerable moment, with transparency, dignity, and genuine choice.

### Core Values
- **Transparency:** No hidden fees, no confusion—just honest prices
- **Dignity:** Respectful, compassionate, never morbid
- **Trust:** Professional, verified, regulated
- **Simplicity:** Complex decisions made clear and manageable
- **Autonomy:** Your choices, your way, your timeline

### Brand Personality
VALE is **professional yet warm**, **authoritative yet accessible**, **modern yet timeless**.

Think: Stripe + Revolut (trustworthy fintech) meets NHS (healthcare authority) meets premium estate planning (respectful elegance).

**NOT:** Death-focused, dark, funeral home stereotypes, corporate coldness, morbid, overly cheerful.

## BRAND VOICE & TONE

### Voice (Who We Are)
- **Honest:** We don't sugarcoat; we give you the full picture
- **Compassionate:** We understand you're grieving; we support you
- **Clear:** Complex things explained simply
- **Empowering:** Your decisions matter; we help you make them well
- **Respectful:** Dignity in every interaction

### Tone (How We Sound)
**By Context:**

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Describing the problem | Validating, not judgmental | "Losing someone is hard. Making funeral decisions shouldn't be." |
| Showing solutions | Confident, reassuring | "See real prices upfront. Choose with confidence." |
| Error messages | Helpful, not blaming | "Oops—postcode not recognized. Try [suggestions]." |
| Call-to-actions | Warm, not pushy | "Plan ahead when you're ready" vs "Don't delay!" |
| Customer stories | Genuine, human | Use real family quotes; avoid corporate speak |

### Language Principles
- **Short sentences.** Long sentences confuse grieving people.
- **Active voice.** "We help you compare" not "Comparisons can be made."
- **Avoid jargon.** Say "price list" not "itemised service offerings."
- **Empathy first.** Lead with understanding, not features.
- **Concrete, not abstract.** "£3,200 for a simple funeral" not "affordable options."

### Example Copy Across Pages

**Landing Page Hero:**
"Compare funeral prices. Choose with confidence."
*(Short, clear, empowering)*

**Family Search Page:**
"Find funeral directors near you. See real prices upfront."
*(Action + benefit)*

**Vault Page:**
"Plan ahead when you're ready. Your wishes, protected."
*(Gentle, not pushy)*

**FD Onboarding:**
"Go live in minutes. Get real families looking for you."
*(Quick, benefit-focused)*

- **Healthcare apps** (calm, trustworthy, professional)

- **Upscale funeral homes** (elegant, dignified, quality-focused)

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Font Family
**Primary: Inter** (via Google Fonts or system default)
- Clean, modern, professional
- Excellent readability at all sizes
- Works perfectly in fintech & healthcare contexts
- Fallback: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif

**Why Inter:** Not Arial, not Roboto, not generic. Inter is distinctive enough to feel premium while remaining highly readable for grieving families who need clarity.

---

### Type Hierarchy Rules

1. **H1 only once per page** - One main title per page for clarity
2. **Clear contrast** - Headers dark (#1a3a52), body dark (#111827), secondary lighter (#6b7280)
3. **Line height breathing room** - 1.6 for body text (helps grieving readers)
4. **Generous margins** - 24px-32px between major sections
5. **Avoid too many font sizes** - Stick to the scale above; don't create new sizes

## SPACING & LAYOUT SYSTEM

### Base Unit
All spacing is based on **8px increments**:
- 4px (micro, rare)
- 8px (tight)
- 12px (small)
- 16px (standard)
- 24px (medium)
- 32px (large)
- 48px (extra large)
- 64px (hero)

### Layout Grid

**Desktop (1200px+):**
```
- 12-column grid
- 24px gutter between columns
- 48px margins (left and right)
```

**Tablet (768px - 1200px):**
```
- 8-column grid
- 16px gutter
- 24px margins
```

**Mobile (< 768px):**
```
- Single column, full width
- 16px padding (left and right)
- 12px gutter between stacked elements
```

### Section Spacing

**Between major sections:** 64px (top and bottom)  
**Between subsections:** 32px  
**Between items in a list:** 16px  
**Padding inside cards:** 24px  
**Padding inside buttons:** 12px horizontal, 12px vertical

## PHOTOGRAPHY & IMAGERY

### Style Guidelines

**DO:**
- Show diverse, real families (multi-generational, multi-ethnic)
- Capture genuine moments (not staged, not overly posed)
- Use warm, natural lighting
- Photograph elderly people with dignity and respect
- Show peaceful, calm environments (gardens, homes, quiet spaces)
- Use high-quality, professional images

**DON'T:**
- Use death/coffin/cemetery imagery
- Show grief-stricken, crying faces
- Use stock photos of "sad people"
- Overly bright, cheerful funeral imagery
- Cartoonish or cutesy depictions of death
- Generic "business team" imagery

### Photography Contexts

| Section | Mood | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Landing hero | Calm, hopeful, inclusive | Multi-generational family in garden |
| FD profiles | Professional, trustworthy | Funeral director in calm, elegant space |
| Vault/planning | Warm, intimate, thoughtful | Family in home setting, looking through documents |
| Success/confirmation | Light, relieved, grateful | Family smiling warmly (not forced glee) |

### Image Technical Specs

- **Format:** WebP (with JPG fallback)
- **Optimization:** Compress to <100KB for web
- **Resolution:** 2x for retina displays
- **Alt text:** Descriptive, meaningful (not "image001.jpg")
- **Aspect ratios:** 16:9 for hero, 1:1 for profiles, 3:2 for cards

## MOTION & ANIMATION

### Principles
- **Purposeful:** Every animation has a reason (attention, transition, feedback)
- **Respectful:** No flashy, distracting animations (people are grieving)
- **Subtle:** Micro-interactions, not dramatic effects
- **Fast:** 200-400ms for most interactions (not slow and heavy)

### Animation Types

#### Page Load (First Paint)
```css
Stagger animations, 100ms between elements:
- Heading fades in (200ms)
- Cards slide up from bottom (300ms, 100ms stagger)
- Buttons fade in (200ms)
Overall mood: Calm reveal, not jarring
```

#### Button Hover
```css
Duration: 150ms
Effect: Slight scale (1 → 1.02), background color shift
Easing: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) (ease-in-out)
```

#### Form Validation
```css
Error shake: 2 shakes over 300ms
Success check: Smooth scale + color fade (200ms)
```

#### List Item Expansion
```css
Max-height animation: 0 → auto over 300ms
Opacity: 0 → 1 over 200ms
```

#### Modal/Dialog Entrance
```css
Background fade-in: 150ms
Content scale + fade: 0.95 → 1 over 200ms
```

### Animation Speed Guidelines
- **Micro-interactions:** 150-200ms (hover states, button feedback)
- **Page transitions:** 300-400ms (fade between pages)
- **Entrance animations:** 300-500ms (staggered reveals)
- **Loading states:** Smooth, continuous (spinners, progress bars)

### Avoid
- Bouncy animations (bounce, elastic easing)
- Auto-playing video/GIFs
- Parallax scrolling (can disorient grieving users)
- Rapid flashing/strobing
- Overly long transitions (>500ms feels sluggish)

## DO'S & DON'TS

### DO ✅

| DO | Why | Example |
|----|-----|---------|
| Use deep blue (#1a3a52) for important elements | Trust, authority | Primary buttons, headings |
| Pair with gold (#d4a574) accents sparingly | Premium, dignity | "Assured" badges, highlights |
| Use plenty of whitespace | Calm, clear | 24px+ margins between sections |
| Keep copy short and simple | Grief affects focus | "£3,200 for a simple funeral" |
| Show diverse families in imagery | Inclusive | Multi-generational, multi-ethnic |
| Use professional photography | Trust | Real people, real environments |
| Test with real funeral directors | Adoption | Get feedback from NAFD/SAIF |
| Ensure mobile responsiveness | Real usage | Test on iPhone/Android |
| Use Inter font consistently | Clean aesthetic | Headings and body text |
| Pair dark text on light backgrounds | Accessible | #111827 on #f9fafb |

### DON'T ❌

| DON'T | Why | Instead |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Use dark, moody backgrounds | Feels morbid, sad | Light, calm backgrounds |
| Show coffins or death imagery | Inappropriate, traumatic | Show peaceful, dignified spaces |
| Use bright, cheerful colors | Feels disrespectful | Use calm, professional palette |
| Make decisions hard to find | People are stressed | Clear navigation, big buttons |
| Use jargon or complex language | Grief reduces comprehension | Simple, direct copy |
| Animate everything | Distracting, unprofessional | Purposeful, subtle motion |
| Use generic stock photos | Feels inauthentic | Real, diverse photography |
| Force decisions with urgency | Manipulative | Give people time and space |
| Use Arial, Roboto, system fonts | Generic, forgettable | Use Inter intentionally |
| Hide pricing information | Exactly what we're fixing | Show all prices upfront |
